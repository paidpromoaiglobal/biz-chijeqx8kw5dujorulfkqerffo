Bridal Makeup Studio

Bridal Makeup Studio, led by the talented Ms. Pushpa, is Chennai's trusted destination for brides who deserve nothing but perfection on their most special days. With a deep understanding of Indian bridal aesthetics and skin tones, Pushpa creates looks that are both timeless and deeply personal.
